About Me

This is who I am.

My name is Davis Herrera. I am a college student at Brigham Young University in pursuit of a Bachelors Degree in Information Systems. I love the outdoors, being adventerous, playing sports, and spending time with my friends and family.

Before going to school, I served a two year, church mission in Honduras. Those two years drastically changed my perspective on life and on education. In a country like Honduras, it is very rare to see a teenager apply for let alone think of college as an option. I am grateful that I have the oportunity to study a subject that I love.

With a degree in Information Systems, I aspire to create good in the world by becoming an entreprenuer. I would love to use my skills to provide tech that helps the lives of others.
